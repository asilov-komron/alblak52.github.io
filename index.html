About
Alblak52 is a rap artist from Russia, Saint-Petersburg. He is well known for his debut album “On the run” which was released in 2022. It gave him a great boost in popularity among his listeners. In summer of this year he released his second solo album (Long live 812) and now he is having a concert tour across 21 cities in Russian Federation.
